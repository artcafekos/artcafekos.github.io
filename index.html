About our cafe

Established in 2015, Art Cafe is a family coffee shop that offers an experience unlike any other in Kos.Our goal is to indulge the senses of all coffee lovers and travelers and offer them a relaxing break in a bubble of coziness

If you're looking for a place to kick start your day, to relax, or you are a coffee enthousiast then Art Cafe is just what you're looking for.
